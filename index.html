The Language Of Flowers


Flowers Meaning
Creme Roses Cream-colored roses symbolize purity, innocence, and charm. They convey admiration and elegance.
Lavender Stock Lavender stock flowers bring a sense of calmness and tranquility. They often represent a lasting bond or a wish for good luck.
White Asiatic Lilies White Asiatic lilies symbolize purity, virtue, and devotion. They are associated with innocence and new beginnings.
Pink Wax flowers Pink wax flowers can represent lasting love, happiness, and contentment. They add a touch of sweetness to arrangements.
Red Roses Red roses are the ultimate symbol of love, passion, and deep affection. They convey strong romantic feelings and desire.
